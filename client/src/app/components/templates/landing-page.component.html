BuffaloFinance

Finance your stocks with our MEAN Stack project. Designed to be a professional-like application to manage your stocks, apply them to portfolios and compare prices. The vision for this project stems from Yahoo!Finance but due to limitations on dynamic updates on current stocks, the project was made to contain static stock data.

Features

See what can be accomplished in this application.

Load any stock

Stocks can be dynamically searched for and you can view them in chart format or look at its details.

Store them into portfolios

Stocks can be made into portfolios which allow you to view a consolidated performance.

Create an account

By signing up, we store your created portfolios into our database.

Compare portfolio performance

Test your portfolios against other stocks and see the portfolio's profits
